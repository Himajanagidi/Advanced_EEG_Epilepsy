About Our System

Advanced AI-powered pediatric EEG analysis for early epilepsy detection

Project Overview

Epilepsy is a major neurological disorder in children, where accurate and early diagnosis is essential for effective treatment. Our project proposes a hybrid quantum–classical method for classifying pediatric EEG signals to detect epilepsy disorders.

Raw EEG recordings are first preprocessed and divided into standard frequency bands, and then the band powers are projected onto scalp topographic maps to generate image-based features that capture spatial activity patterns across the brain.

A lightweight convolutional neural network (CNN) is employed to learn visual features from these maps, and the resulting embeddings are further reduced and classified using a Quantum Support Vector Machine (QSVM) with amplitude embedding.
